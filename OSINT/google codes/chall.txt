# Lost Coordinates

Legends speak of a mark left upon every corner of the world, a silent signature woven into the fabric of the maps we trust every day.
Most walk over it without a second thought.
Others spend their entire lives searching for clues hidden in plain sight.
Yet for those who know where to look, the mark reveals itself: a unique code, bound to a single place and known only to those who seek it.
Some say it was created to help travelers find their way.
Others believe it is a modern relic, a digital coordinate etched into the landscape itself.
Whatever the truth may be, one such code lies closer than you imagine—so close that every step of your journey begins from it.

Your mission is simple: discover the **Google Plus Code** of the place legends speak about.
Once found, enclose it within the sacred flag format:

`VBCTF{PLUS_CODE}`

No cryptography guards this secret. No firewall stands in your path. The answer has existed in plain sight from the very beginning, hidden among countless locations scattered across the world.

Look not to distant lands or forgotten ruins. The coordinates you seek have been beneath your feet all along.

The mark awaits.

Good luck, explorer.
