The Archivist's Ledger

For decades, the kingdom's Grand Archivist recorded every secret, 
every treaty, and every whispered rumor in a magical ledger.
The ledger was peculiar.
No matter how much information was written into it,
the Archivist insisted that each page had a fixed amount of space.
Most scribes respected this rule and wrote only what would fit.

One ambitious apprentice, however, began to wonder what would happen if a message exceeded the space that had been reserved for it.
Would the extra ink simply vanish?

Or would it spill into places it was never meant to reach?
One evening, a strange entry was discovered. The ledger behaved unexpectedly, and passages that should have remained hidden suddenly came into view.

The Archivist dismissed it as an accident.
You suspect otherwise.

Examine the ledger carefully. Sometimes a secret is not found by reading between the lines, but by writing beyond them.
