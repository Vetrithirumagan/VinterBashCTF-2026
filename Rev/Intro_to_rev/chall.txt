The Forgotten Password

Deep beneath an abandoned research facility,
investigators discovered a terminal that had remained locked for years.

According to old records,one of the lead engineers was obsessed with secrecy.
Rather than storing valuable information in plain sight,
he protected it behind a custom authentication program of his own design.

Time has not been kind to the system.
Documentation is gone, backups are corrupted, and nobody remembers the password.
All that remains is the executable itself.

The engineers who built the terminal were confident that no outsider could ever uncover its secret.
After all, how could someone learn the correct password without knowing it beforehand?

Yet confidence often leads to mistakes.

Somewhere within the program lies everything you need to gain access.
The password may not be as hidden as its creator believed,
and the reward waiting behind it is worth the effort.

Your mission is simple:

Analyze the program, discover how it verifies input, and recover the secret hidden within.

Good luck, investigator. The terminal has been waiting a long time for someone curious enough to look inside.
