The Last Box

An old collector was famous for a peculiar habit.
Whenever he wished to protect something valuable, he would place it inside a box.
Then he would place that box inside another.
And then another.
Visitors who discovered his collection often celebrated too early. They would open a box, find something inside, and assume they had reached the treasure.
Yet the collector was always one step ahead.
The true prize was never kept in the outer container.

The artifact before you comes from his private collection. At first glance, it appears to be the answer you seek. Look closer. Certain markings on the box suggest it was prepared using a familiar method of concealment.

Remember: finding a box is not the same as finding the treasure.
Keep opening what you discover until there is nothing left to open.
Only then will you reach the collector's secret.

Submit the final flag exactly as you find it.
